Nutrition

Carbohydrates

Carbohydrates are an important group of foods for fueling your muscles. Carbs are partially converted to glycogen, which is a form of energy stored in muscles. This energy helps to power your workouts. Men and women need about half of their calories from carbohydrates per day but, if you are trying to bulk up then you should be eating about 4-7 grams of carbs per every kilogram of body weight

Protein

Protein is a macronutrient that is essential to building muscle mass. It is commonly found in animal products but also found in other foods such as beans, eggs, nuts, and milk.
